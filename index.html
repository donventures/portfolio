Case Study 01

Kōhii Coffee ☕

A modern Japanese-inspired café concept built for community — students, families, and coffee lovers seeking a chill, cozy space to slow down, connect, and enjoy quality coffee every day.

🇵🇭 Philippines 📱 Social Media ⚡ Automation 🎨 Branding

🔴 The Problem

A local café had zero online presence — no Facebook page, no content, no way for customers to find them or leave feedback. They needed everything built from scratch without requiring daily owner involvement.

✅ The Solution

Created the Facebook business page from scratch — designed the brand kit, set up the full page structure, and launched a daily content strategy around food photography and the café's story. Built a Zapier-powered feedback automation system integrated with Google Sheets for real-time customer response capture.

🏆 The Outcome

Grew the page from 0 to 100+ followers in under 2 weeks — no paid ads, brand new page, no existing audience. Publishing 7–14 posts per week consistently. Customer feedback systems are live and actively capturing responses, giving the café owner real-time visibility for the first time.

Case Study 02

Jinju Milktea 🧋

A local milk tea and food shop that needed a stronger brand identity and better visual marketing to attract more walk-in customers and stand out to a younger, modern audience.

🇵🇭 Philippines 🎨 Brand Redesign 🖨 Print Design 📱 Social Media

🔴 The Problem

Inconsistent visuals and weak brand presentation across all materials. The menu lacked structure, promotional materials had no unified style, and the branding didn't reflect the shop's target audience — making it difficult to build brand recall.

✅ The Solution

Developed a minimalist, visually consistent design direction focused on clarity, modern aesthetics, and promotional visibility. Redesigned menus, created cohesive promotional posters, tarpaulin layouts, and branded social media graphics with consistent typography and a modern visual identity.

🏆 The Outcome

Updated branding improved customer perception and promotional effectiveness. Increased dine-in and takeout orders during promotional campaigns. Stronger visual consistency across menus, posters, and social media materials, and better promotional visibility for new drinks and offers.

Content Strategy & Social Media Workflow

A systematic, repeatable approach that keeps brands consistent, visible, and growing without daily owner stress.

📅 Planning & Scheduling

Content calendars planned 2–4 weeks in advance using Notion. Posts scheduled through Later or Buffer to maintain consistent daily presence, including 7–14 posts per week across platforms.

🎨 Visual Content Creation

Every post is paired with custom branded visuals created in Canva, Adobe Photoshop, or CorelDraw. Food photography direction, story graphics, and promotional designs all follow a unified brand system for maximum recognition.

💬 Community Engagement

Active comment management, automated comment replies via GoHighLevel, and real-time monitoring to build genuine community connections that drive loyalty and repeat visits.

⚡ Automation & Systems

Zapier workflows and GoHighLevel automations handle feedback collection, lead capture, and repetitive tasks — freeing up owner time while keeping operations running smoothly and data flowing into Google Sheets.

📋 My Content Workflow

1
Brand Discovery & Audit

Understand the brand voice, target audience, competitors, and current online presence to build a strategic foundation.

2
Content Strategy Creation

Develop a month-long content calendar in Notion with content types, themes, posting frequency, and engagement goals.

3
Visual Design & Copywriting

Create branded graphics in Canva/Photoshop and write compelling captions aligned to brand voice and audience.

4
Scheduling & Automation

Schedule content through Later or Buffer. Set up GoHighLevel and Zapier automations for comments, feedback, and leads.

5
Monitoring & Optimization

Track engagement metrics via Meta Business Suite, optimize posting times, and refine strategy based on real performance data.
